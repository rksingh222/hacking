Capturing the Handshake
WPA/WPA2 Cracking
1. Fixed all weaknesses in WEP
2. Packets contain no useful data
3. Only packets that can aid with the cracking process are the handshake packets
4. These handshake are 4 packets sent when a client connect to the network

In terminal
First press airodump-ng

In same terminal
airodump-ng --bssid 00:10:18:90:2D:EE --channel 1 --write wpa_handshake mon0
Hit enter
Wait for another client to enter and then we will see the handshake
Alternatively there is another option we can do deauthentication attack.
We can disconnect this client from network. He will automatically connects when the attack stops.
Therefore when he connects again then we will get the handshake

In another terminal
aireplay-ng --deauth 4 -a 00:10:18:90:2D:EE(bssid) -c 80:E6:50:22:A2:E8(client or station) mon0
Hit enter
Once you do this you will show handshake in the first terminal
Then we can quit the command by pressing CTRL +C
